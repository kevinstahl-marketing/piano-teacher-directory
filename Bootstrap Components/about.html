About Us

Connecting students with exceptional piano teachers since 2015

Our Mission

We believe that everyone should have access to quality music education. Our platform connects aspiring pianists with experienced, passionate teachers who can help them achieve their musical goals.

Whether you're a complete beginner taking your first steps into the world of piano, or an advanced student looking to refine your technique, we're here to help you find the perfect teacher match.

What We Value

Quality Education

All teachers in our directory are carefully vetted for their experience, qualifications, and teaching ability.

Student Success

We're committed to helping students of all ages and skill levels discover the joy of playing piano.

Community

We foster a supportive community that celebrates musical growth and the teacher-student relationship.

Our Story

Piano Teachers Directory was founded in 2015 by a group of music educators who recognized the need for a better way to connect students with qualified piano teachers. As teachers themselves, they understood the challenges both students and instructors face in finding the right match.

What started as a small local network has grown into a comprehensive platform serving communities across the country. Today, we're proud to feature hundreds of talented teachers representing diverse teaching styles, musical backgrounds, and specializations.

Our platform continues to evolve based on feedback from both students and teachers, but our core mission remains the same: making quality piano education accessible to everyone.
